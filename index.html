What Are Fan Edits?

Fan edits are short, stylish videos fans create to celebrate TV shows, movies, or celebrities.


See Example


What Does An Edit Include?


Intro:
A full scene with the subject

Part 1:
Short clips of your subject

Part 2:
Often repeats or builds on Part 1

Outro:
Ends the edit, usually as a fade-out or blank screen

Themes & Edit Audios


Themes: Sad, happy, funny, romantic ("ship edit"), powerful, etc.

Audios: Songs with voice-overs and sound effects. Choose audio to match your theme!


Scene Packs


What are they? themed packs of scenes/clips of different characters.

Where can I find them? Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok. (e.g., search "mcu scenepacks" for marvel scene packs)

  • Most accounts only accept other editing accounts.

How do I download them? Usually through Mega or Google Drive. (free!)


Where Can I Edit?


Laptop/Desktop: After Effects, with Adobe. (if you're in school, possible discount!)

Mobile Device: Options include Cap Cut, and Video Star (which I'll give a brief intro to)

*Video Star is only available on Apple devices.
