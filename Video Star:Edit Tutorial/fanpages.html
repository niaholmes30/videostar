Make your Fan Page!


Choose Your Platform!

The most common platforms to post edits on are TikTok and Instagram, and I suggest you post on both to reach a broader audience.

Set Up Your Account!

Create a separate email to make an account with.

Usernames can be related to your fandom, or whatever you like! My username is theromanticized

If you're stuck, you can find username ideas on Pinterest, or even ask generative AI to make one up for you!

Who To Follow First?

Follow whoever you'd like! Make sure to follow other editors, as well as audio and scene pack accounts.

You also want to be really active on editor accounts (liking and commenting), especially with your mutuals (but don't spam!)

Post!

Post as much as you can in the beginning! After you have more followers, you'll be able to analyze your account analytics and determine when your followers are most active.

Captions & Hashtags

Include a fun statement to engage with your audience, credits to other accounts if you used their work (audios, scene packs, presets, etc.), and hashtags.
