My Experience & Advice

I started editing during the Pandemic, so I had a lot of time learn and troubleshoot the whole editing process. That's the main thing I want to highlight: editing is going to take time, so be patient with yourself and don't give up! I am still a learning things myself, and I have been editing for over 4 years now!

Remember to have consistency! My current editing account does not display 4 years of growth because I switched editing accounts so often (do not do that!) This entire process will be extremely stressful, but you will always be proud of the end results, and you'll have fun if it's something you really like doing.
