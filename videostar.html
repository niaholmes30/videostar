What Is Video Star?

Video Star (VS) is a mobile app used for creating high-quality video edits. Video Star offers a free and pro version.


Use the free version first to get used to the basic features then move on to Pro.

Pro Version: 1 month for $4.99 OR 1 year for $29.99
